Installing the server

 

The server is installed with an installer. Just double click on the downloaded file and choose a location. The rest is pretty much straightforward.

 

Please take note of the license and the password!!! If you forgot to take note of the password: open the server.log in the map you installed TeamSpeak to. There will be some lines telling you your Superadmin password and your normal password.
