Your IP’s

 

With this function you can find out what IP’s are used in your network. This function is especially useful for people with a dynamic IP. When clicking it, it will show your internal IP (if you have one it will be in the range of 10.x.x.x, 192.168.x.x) and the external IP.

People connecting to your server need to know the external IP. This external IP needs to be forwarded to your local computer. How this is done is however out of the scope of this manual. Refer to your router’s manual or ask your network administrator about port forwarding.

Note that this function can be disabled. Take a look at the Main Config section of this manual for more information.
